About us?


At Tolosaldea Machinery, we are proud to be leaders in the precision machining sector, offering innovative and high-quality solutions to satisfy the most demanding needs of our customers. With an accumulated experience of more than 10 years in the industry, we have established ourselves as a reliable and efficient reference in the world of machining.


CNC means "Computer Numerical Control". It is an automated system for the manufacturing and machining of parts. Instead of relying on manual intervention, CNC machines are controlled by commands programmed into software, allowing for precise and repeatable operations, increasing efficiency and production speed. They are widely used in the manufacturing industry.

Machinery electronics involves the use of electronic components and systems to control and improve the operation of industrial machinery. Electronic circuits, sensors and control systems are used to automate processes, adjust parameters and improve efficiency. From simple systems to advanced solutions with microcontrollers and PLCs, electronics offer flexibility and efficiency in various industrial sectors.

Updating machinery involves incorporating improvements into existing equipment to optimize performance and functionality. This may include the integration of more advanced technologies, replacement of obsolete components or implementation of new features to improve productivity and extend the useful life of equipment, providing economic and operational benefits to companies.!
